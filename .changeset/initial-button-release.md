Initial public release. Adds the `Button` component: six interactive states (idle, hover, active, focus-visible, disabled, loading), `loading`/`loadingIndicator`/`startIcon`/`endIcon` props, three sizes and three intents, and CSS-variable theming through the zero-specificity override contract. Server-renderable — no `"use client"`. Ships a precompiled `styles.css`.
